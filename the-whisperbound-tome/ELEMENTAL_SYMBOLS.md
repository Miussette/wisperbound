# Elemental Symbols Guide

Each spell category in The Whisperbound Tome is associated with an elemental force and has its own unique symbol.

## The Eight Elements

### ⊙ Protection - Earth
**Color:** Brown (#8B4513)
**Element:** Earth
**Description:** Shield of stone and root
**Keywords:** Safe, guard, shield, ward, protect, defend

### ◐ Revelation - Light
**Color:** Gold (#FFD700)
**Element:** Light
**Description:** Eye of illumination
**Keywords:** See, reveal, show, discover, uncover, illuminate

### ⋔ Binding - Iron
**Color:** Slate Gray (#708090)
**Element:** Iron
**Description:** Chains of will
**Keywords:** Bind, hold, tie, fasten, secure, lock

### △ Transformation - Fire
**Color:** Orange Red (#FF4500)
**Element:** Fire
**Description:** Flame of change
**Keywords:** Change, transform, shift, alter, become, evolve

### ◇ Summoning - Air
**Color:** Sky Blue (#87CEEB)
**Element:** Air
**Description:** Breath of calling
**Keywords:** Call, summon, invoke, bring, gather, draw

### ◢ Banishment - Void
**Color:** Indigo (#4B0082)
**Element:** Void
**Description:** Gate of dismissal
**Keywords:** Banish, dismiss, expel, remove, cast out, send away

### ◆ Preservation - Crystal
**Color:** Turquoise (#40E0D0)
**Element:** Crystal
**Description:** Amber of eternity
**Keywords:** Preserve, keep, maintain, sustain, hold, endure

### ◌ Passage - Water
**Color:** Steel Blue (#4682B4)
**Element:** Water
**Description:** Current of transition
**Keywords:** Pass, cross, travel, journey, move, transition

## Usage in Custom Spells

When creating your own spells, select the elemental category that best matches your intent. The symbol will appear on your spell card and glow with its associated color.

Each element has unique properties:
- **Earth** spells are grounding and protective
- **Light** spells reveal hidden truths
- **Iron** spells bind and constrain
- **Fire** spells transform and change
- **Air** spells call and summon
- **Void** spells banish and dismiss
- **Crystal** spells preserve and maintain
- **Water** spells facilitate passage and transition

### What is it?
Short, beautiful breathing practices (4-8 minutes) designed to help you:
- Ground when overwhelmed
- Release what no longer serves
- Find energy when depleted
- Discover peace in stillness
- Gain clarity in confusion

### How to use:
1. Navigate to "Guided Rituals" from the sidebar
2. Choose a ritual that matches your current need
3. Click "begin ritual" to enter the sanctuary
4. Follow the gentle instructions
5. Watch the breathing circle expand and contract
6. Let the ritual guide your breath

### Available Rituals:

#### ‚äô Roots of Earth (5 min)
**Category:** Grounding  
**When to use:** When the world feels too much  
**Breath pattern:** 4-2-6-2 (inhale-hold-exhale-pause)  
Imagine roots growing from your feet into dark, rich soil

#### ‚óå Current of Release (7 min)
**Category:** Release  
**When to use:** To let go of what no longer serves  
**Breath pattern:** 4-4-6-2, then 4-2-8-2  
Visualize a gentle stream carrying away all that weighs you down

#### ‚ñ≥ Flame of Vitality (4 min)
**Category:** Energy  
**When to use:** When energy feels low  
**Breath pattern:** 2-1-3-1, then 3-2-4-1  
Kindle the flame that burns at your core

#### ‚ó¢ Sanctuary of Silence (8 min)
**Category:** Peace  
**When to use:** To find stillness between thoughts  
**Breath pattern:** 5-3-7-3  
Enter the quiet void where peace dwells

#### ‚óê Illumination of Mind (6 min)
**Category:** Clarity  
**When to use:** When decisions feel overwhelming  
**Breath pattern:** 4-4-6-2  
Light pierces through confusion, revealing what is true

### The Breathing Circle

During rituals, you'll see a glowing circle that:
- **Expands** during inhale
- **Holds steady** during the hold phase
- **Contracts** during exhale
- **Rests** during the pause

The circle's color matches the ritual's element, creating a visual anchor for your practice.

## üí´ Tips for Use

### For Oracle Mode:
- Don't rush to answer the prompt
- Let it sit with you throughout the day
- Journal about it if that feels right
- Draw another when you're ready

### For Guided Rituals:
- Find a quiet space where you won't be disturbed
- Use headphones for better immersion
- Don't worry about "doing it right"
- If your mind wanders, that's okay - gently return
- You can end early if needed - honor your needs

### Integration:
- Use Oracle Mode when journaling feels stuck
- Try a ritual before important decisions
- Practice grounding before stressful events
- Use release rituals after difficult days
